    • Bitcoin: Brr Money and Digital Gold
      • Real BTC story: central banks added trillions of dollars and monetized sovereign debt; real yields are negative with record debt levels. MMT became policy in 2020.
      • If Democrats win the runoff elections in January, MMT will be unopposed. If Congress stays divided, the money printer will go brr. In both cases, BTC stands to benefit.
      • BTC is now prominent in the macroeconomic conversation; looks like the smart money alternative to gold.
    • Ethereum: the Everything Marketplace
      • If BTC is primed for a big year due to the macro, Ethereum has become the most important platform. It can both power a new financial system and build a more open/resilient internet.
      • Economic value transacted via ETH is already more than that of BTC.
    • DeFi: a Bankless Future
      • The building blocks of a new financial system: payments, lending, insurance, asset issuance/exchange.
      • Only way to slow this down would be a regulatory crackdown.
