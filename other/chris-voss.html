Chris Voss Masterclass

  • Mirroring
    • O: I like it at this yoga resort
    • Me: At this yoga resort?
  • Labelling
    • It seems like …
    • It sounds like …
      • Let it sink in
  • Delivery
    • Everyone likes deference
    • Ditch the assertive voice
      • Fight guy — direct and blunt
    • Use the playful voice 80% of the time
    • Analyst voice
      • Downward, slow, soothing tone, no warmth
    • Late-night FM voice
    • Digital communication
      • Never rely on one form of communication
      • Always end positively
      • In an email, make sure words land softly
  • Body language
    • 7/38/55 rule
      • 7% content
      • 38% tone
      • 55% body language
    • Watch the sidelines
      • People you are not speaking to will be very honest in their bodily reactions
    • Identify baselines and spot deviations
    • Pinnochio effect
      • If someone is speaking with many words, they are likely lying
