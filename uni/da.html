Task 2

Ni, Pi, NPi, Lower

  • Ni
    • Number of occurrences of phenomena
  • Pi
    • Fair distribution (1/5=0.2)
  • NPi
    • Fair distribution * number of occurrences
  • Lower
    • ((Ni-NPi)^2)/NPi

Observed Value & Critical Value & Degs of Freedom

  • Observed Value
    • Sum of all Lower amounts
  • Degrees of Freedom
    • Types of phenomena -1
  • Critical Value
    • =CHISQ.INV.RT(Alfa,DegFree)
